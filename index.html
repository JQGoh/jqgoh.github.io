About

I am an academic researcher turned data scientist looking to solve complex problems beyond the field of natural sciences. As a computational physicist, I specialized in studying the fundamental properties of nanoparticles by simulating their quantum chemical properties and visualizing their atomic structures. Upon my academic career, I ventured into public transportation domain and my current research focuses on the optimization of real-time bus scheduling. I am keen to leverage on data and engineering technologies to address social challenges. Other than these geeky stuff, I would love to spend more time on practising Tai Chi, swimming and playing Xiangqi.
